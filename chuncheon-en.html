Introduction

    Overview

    Chuncheon is located at the confluence of the North Han River and its tributary Shaoyang River, in the middle of the Chuncheon Basin, about 100 kilometers away from Seoul. As the capital of Gangwon Province, Chuncheon is both the administrative center of Gangwon Province and a famous tourist attraction. The main industries are rice milling, textiles, paper making, and automobile repairs

    As a famous tourist attraction, Chuncheon is closely related to Korean dramas. The beautiful and charming love drama "Winter Sonata" was also shot here on Nami Island. The fresh and bright scene attracted many fans to travel to Chuncheon. Chuncheon has the famous snow scene in Korea, and the sad and quiet scenes of snowfall in the play have become classic scenes in people's hearts. The two rows of birch forests on the island have now become a splendid love pilgrimage site in South Korea, attracting many young couples.

    The famous scenic spots in Chuncheon include Gangchon, Nami Island, Jungdo Island, Cheongpyeongsa Temple, Kongzhicheon, Rongdo Island, Soyang, Amusement Park, Yamanashi Village, Samaksan Mountain, Dengxian Waterfall, Kugu Waterfall, Chuncheon Hunting Ground, etc. Chuncheon Lake has a quiet environment and beautiful scenery. It is also a great place for family picnics and lovers to date. When you arrive at Chuncheon, don't forget to taste the delicious Chuncheon soba noodles and fried chicken steak. The festivals here are colorful and full of fun. Traveling in the festive month will surely reap unexpected happiness, and it is known as "Korea's most livable city".

    Culture

    Korean culture is recorded in history in 35 BC. The North-South confrontation on the Korean peninsula after the Korean War led to differences in the modern culture of South Korea, but the traditional culture between the two sides is deeply rooted. Once the Korean Peninsula and China had very close trade and cultural exchanges, the Korean Peninsula was deeply influenced by Chinese traditional culture, and it still retains and promotes its own traditional culture with its uniqueness. Since 2000, the meteor storm of Korean popular entertainment has swept across Asia, and many Korean idol groups have appeared frequently, driving tens of thousands of teenagers crazy.

    Environment

    In recent years, Chuncheon has always regarded itself as the "best place for investment with a good business environment". The natural and beautiful natural environment and popularity in recent years are the advantages of Chuncheon, and it is also the most desirable place to live in the country selected by Koreans. Ranked first in the city. "Good mountain, good water, good forest" is the most appropriate interpretation of Chuncheon.

Tourist time

    Tourist season

    Chuncheon is located inland and has obvious characteristics of the inland climate. The temperature difference is large, the annual average temperature is 10 degrees Celsius, but in winter it has reached extreme weather below minus 20 degrees.

    Spring (March to March): Chuncheon is warm and comfortable at this time, and everything recovers. The most suitable activity is to invite a group of friends out to enjoy the flowers and barbecue on Nami Island. However, the temperature difference between morning and evening in the mountains and forests is large, so appropriate clothing should be added.

    Summer (June-August): This is a season when locals will choose to go out on vacation. However, July in South Korea is generally the rainy season with heavy rainfall. Tourists should take care to bring umbrellas.

    Autumn (September-November): The temperature is mild and the sky is clear. Chuncheon is very beautiful at this time. The red maple leaves on Nami Island are very attractive.

    Winter (December-February): The snow scene of Nami Island in winter is the business card of Chuncheon, and it is also the reason for tourists to visit especially. The temperature in winter is very low and it is very beautiful when it snows. Tourists can choose to come to Chuncheon at this time.

    Best time to travel

    Due to the basin topography of Chuncheon, the region has obvious inland climate characteristics. The winter is relatively cold, suitable for sightseeing in spring and autumn. And there are more festivals at this time, which will make the trip more interesting.
