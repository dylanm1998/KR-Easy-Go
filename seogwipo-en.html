Introduction

    Overview

    Seogwipo is the southernmost city in South Korea and is the most popular fishing and diving destination along with Jeju City. Approximately 4 million tourists visit each year. It is an international tourist hotspot. With an average annual temperature of 15.7°C and an average annual rainfall of 1675.7 mm, it is the warmest place in Korea with the highest rainfall.

Tourist time

    Tourist season

    Jeju Island is located on the east side of the Asian continent and is heavily influenced by the monsoon. At the same time, it is surrounded by the sea and presents an oceanic climate. In addition, the unique subtropical humid climate of Jeju Island is formed due to the influence of Halla Mountain which is located in the center of the island at an altitude of 1950 meters and the offshore currents. Jeju Island is dry and windy in winter, wet and rainy in summer. The annual average temperature is around 16°C, the highest temperature in summer is 33.5°C, and the lowest temperature in winter is 1°C.

    Spring (April-June mid-June): Cool and comfortable. At this time, all flowers bloom and the scenery is beautiful.

    Summer (late June to early September): It is a season with multiple typhoons, with concentrated rainfall. Days without typhoons are suitable for swimming in the sea and experiencing various marine sports.

    Autumn (Mid-September-Early November): Autumn is high and refreshing, usually in late October, the maple leaves of Halla Mountain begin to turn red.

    Winter (Mid-November-March of the following year): The temperature difference is large, the weather is dry, and the sea breeze is strong. It is suitable for visiting indoor attractions.

    Best time to travel

    The temperature of Jeju Island from March to April changes relatively greatly, and the temperature in high-altitude areas is relatively low. It is not a good time to travel until May. In June, the hydrangea blooms along the roads and the seaside, and many valleys welcome tourists. The arrival of the small volcano in October is the best month to travel to Seogwipo.
