Introduction

    Overview

    Ansan City is a new rural industrial city. Ansan City plays a very important role in Korea's economic development. It has two industrial complexes, Banyue and Sihwa, and its scale ranks first in the country. Most of the companies in the industrial complex have strong technical levels in Korea and the world market, and many of them are supporting enterprises of well-known large companies. , Directly provide various parts and components for it, the main industries are fiber, chemistry, electronics, metal machining, and printing, etc.

    Culture

    Ansan is a new industrial city and the most concentrated area of small and medium-sized enterprises. It is known as "the cradle of Korean small and medium-sized enterprises."

    Environment

    Ansan City is located in the southwest within 30㎞ from Seoul, adjacent to the west coast, and the terrain tends to slope slowly toward the inland area. Adjacent to Siheung City, Gwangdeok Mountain, and Masan Mountain is in the north and low hills and mountains such as Luofeng Mountain and Qibao Mountain in the south. In addition, rivers such as the Banyue River, Anshan River, and Huating River span the urban area, and large alluvial lands are formed around the rivers. Most of these alluvial lands are used for farming.

Tourist time

    Tourist season

    Ansan City is located in a temperate continental climate zone, with an average annual temperature of 12 degrees Celsius; the average temperature in January is -3 degrees Celsius, and the average temperature in August is 25.5 degrees Celsius.

    Spring (March to March): At this time, Ansan City is warm and comfortable, and everything is recovering. However, the temperature difference between morning and evening is large, so you should add clothes appropriately, and avoid traveling in yellow sand weather.

    Summer (June-August): July is generally the rainy season, with heavy rainfall, visitors should bring umbrellas with them.

    Autumn (September-November): The temperature is mild, and the autumn is high and refreshing. It is the most suitable time to visit Ansan City.

    Winter (December-February): The temperature in Ansan City is low in winter, so tourists should take measures to keep warm.

    Best time to travel

    Ansan City is a city dominated by industry, but every May there is a special international street art festival, which is very lively and very interesting.
