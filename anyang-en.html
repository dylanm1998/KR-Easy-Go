Introduction

    Overview

    Anyang City is well-known for its 8 scenic spots in Anyang, namely, the sunset at Manghae-an on Gwanaksan Mountain, Geunseok at Sammoksa Temple, Pyeongchon Central Park, Sammacheon Manan Bridge, Mt. Choi Gyeonghwan Sacred Site, Anyang Art Park, Nongjeongri Forest Bath Changshi Pagoda, and Anyang No. 1 Street are the most famous tourist destinations in the city. Repair Mountain is located at the junction of Anyang City, Gunpo City, and Anshan City. It is 489 meters above sea level. From the top of the mountain, you can see a panoramic view of Anyang City. It is surrounded by Gwanak Mountain, Xiuam Mountain, and Junjabong. Sansheng Mountain is a mountain suitable for mountaineering. Legend has it that Master Yuan Xiao, together with Yixiang and Yin Bi, built and practiced the Three Acts Temple, so this mountain is called "Sansheng Mountain." Many historical sites in Anyang City are listed as cultural properties of Gyeonggi Province, including Wananqiao, the three-story stone pagoda of Sammakusa Temple, the site of a pottery kiln in Bisandong, the three-story stone pagoda at Jongchusa Temple, and the monument of Sammakusa Temple.

    Culture

    The name Anyang comes from the Anyangsa Temple built by the King King King of Goryeo. In the 4th year of King Hyogung of Silla (900), Wang Jian, a descendant of Gongye, passed the Three Sacred Mountains to conquer places such as Jinju (Siheung) and Gwju (Gwacheon). At this time, seeing the clouds on the top of the mountain glow with colorful lights, I felt very strange and sent people to check it out. Under Caiyun, I met an old monk named Nengzheng. What the old monk said was the same as Wang Jian thought, so he built Anyang Temple here (in the area of No. 2 Shishui 1 Dongshan, Wan'an District). In the Joseon Dynasty, King Jeongjo added the word "An" to the word "An" in the Wanan Bridge built for his father to mourn his son's trip. The word "nurturing" may be created to allow future generations to remember the foundation of human relations-"filial piety". "Anyang" in Buddhism means the pure land of bliss, a world where everything is perfect, there is no pain, only happiness, freedom, and warmth.

    Environment

    There is Xiuli Mountain in the southwest of Anyang, Gwanak Mountain and Samseong Mountain in the north, and an oval basin in the center. Anyang is located in the southwestern part of Seoul and is a developed city in the middle and upper reaches of the Anyang River, a tributary of the Han River. People settled down in the Anyang River Basin and used the Anyang River water to develop agriculture and industry. Compared with other cities, Anyang's industrial development has been relatively early, and the construction of roads extending in all directions has played a significant role in the development of the local area.

Tourist time

    Tourist season

    Anyang City has four distinct seasons. Generally, winter is cold and dry, and summer is hot and humid. The annual average temperature is 12.3℃, and the annual average precipitation is 1,198mm, most of which are concentrated between June and August.

    Spring (March to March): At this time, Anyang City is warm and comfortable, and everything recovers. However, the temperature difference between morning and evening is large, so you should add clothes appropriately, and avoid traveling in yellow sand weather.

    Summer (June-August): July is generally the rainy season, with heavy rainfall, visitors should bring umbrellas with them.

    Autumn (September-November): The temperature is mild and the autumn is high and refreshing, it is the most suitable time to visit Anyang City.

    Winter (December-February): The temperature is low in winter, so tourists should take measures to keep warm.

    Best time to travel

    In autumn, the air is high and the temperature is good. It is most suitable for visiting Anyang City, watching the eight scenic spots of Anyang, and climbing high and looking into the distance.
