Introduction

    Overview

    Daegu is located in Gyeongsangbuk-do, South Korea, in the southeast of Seoul, adjacent to the bay city of Pohang in the east, and Busan in the south. It is the fourth largest city in Korea. A place where tradition and cutting-edge future meet. The source of Yeongnam, the umbilical cord of Nakdong River and Daegu, the Dagupan Plain where the water of Geumho River flows freely, Palgong Mountain, which has always been a leisure place for Daegu citizens; Mount AP and Biseul Mountain, which are located in the outskirts, are convenient for citizens to visit. painting. The ecological rivers and rivers in the city and lush forests and gem-like parks provide people with comfortable urban spaces. Daegu's light industry is well-developed, dominated by textiles, including cotton spinning, wool spinning, silk, chemical fiber, etc., as well as metallurgy, machinery, rubber, and other industries. Daegu City has a subtropical climate, so the apples and melons are very sweet. Held the 2011 World Athletics Championships. Daegu has many famous tourist attractions, including the medicinal materials market, tumulus park, etc.

Tourist time

    Tourist season

    Daegu is surrounded by mountains, and the temperature difference is large between cold winter and hot summer. It was once rated as the hottest city in South Korea, but the average temperature has dropped year by year due to large-scale urban greening in recent years. In the last 30 years, the average annual temperature has been 13.7°C.

    Best time to travel

    The spring and autumn seasons are the most suitable to visit Daegu, the weather is warm and dry, and the sun is also very abundant.
