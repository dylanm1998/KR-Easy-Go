Introduction

    Overview

    Incheon is a marine tourism city, including more than a hundred large and small islands such as Ganghwa Island and Baengnyeong Island, forming a unique natural landscape. At the same time, the historical and cultural resources from the prehistoric period to the modern opening period are left here, forming a strong cultural tourism resource. In addition, there are Incheon International Airport and Incheon Port, which are important ports connecting various regions around the world, Korea's first free economic zone, Songdo International City, which showcases the concept of a high-tech future city for the world, and Incheon landmark buildings. The Incheon Bridge shows the colorful characteristics and charm of Incheon. In 2014, the 17th Asian Games was held here.

    The greatest comfort in Incheon comes from the fresh air and healthy food. In addition, the warmth and thoughtfulness of the local citizens also make people feel very warm. Due to its special relationship with the ocean, Incheon has become a marine tourism city that everyone loves. There are delicious seafood dishes, romantic islands, and a variety of festivals, whether it is entertainment facilities on land or the ocean. All of the experience programs in Incheon can make people living in Incheon feel full of enjoyment. Even outsiders can live with the locals in a fun way.

    Culture

    Incheon is an important port for the Republic of Korea to absorb the essence of foreign culture, and it can be said that it is the gate of Korean foreign culture. Incheon City is located in the central and western parts of the Korean Peninsula. It was the first city in South Korea to embark on the path of modernization at the end of the 19th century, and it is also South Korea's second-largest port city. Wolmi Island and the coastal wharf, which have been baptized by history and years, are the most representative attractions in Incheon, and they have also witnessed the growth and prosperity of Incheon. In modern times, large-scale buildings such as Incheon International Airport, Incheon Bridge, and Songdo International City, a free economic zone, have shown a brand new modern appearance in Incheon. Modern Incheon is a necessary place for South Korea to open to the outside world. It is also one of the enthusiastic cities for foreigners to settle in South Korea. It also contributes to the current multicultural atmosphere.

    Environment

    Incheon City covers an area of 1040.82 square kilometers, and is the third-largest city in South Korea. In terms of climatic environment, Incheon is located in the coastal area, but the climate is a bit dry, and the wind direction in some areas is changeable, which is greatly affected by the season. The average annual temperature in Incheon is 11.4 degrees Celsius, and the average annual temperature difference is 28 degrees Celsius. Geographically, Incheon has more than 150 large and small islands, including the romantic Wolmi Island, Ganghwa Island with rich historical and cultural heritage, and Yeongjong Island where Incheon International Airport is located.

Tourist time

    Tourist season

    Spring: March to May. Incheon has a warm and humid climate in spring with spring flowers in full bloom. This season is very suitable for outdoor activities. On weekends, locals will go on an outing on the island and experience various entertainment facilities. There is a big temperature difference between morning and evening in spring, so you need to prepare a thinner long-sleeved shirt to prevent cold.

    Summer: June to August. Incheon is very hot and humid in summer. Ultraviolet rays are very strong when noon is the hottest. It is necessary to minimize going out to prevent sun damage or heat stroke. There are many interesting festivals on the island in summer, and they are also widely loved by locals. Come to Incheon in summer, you may wish to experience it.

    Autumn: September-November. The air in Incheon in autumn is refreshing. In Korea, autumn is a good season to go out. In Incheon, in addition to walking on the island on weekends, you can also choose to climb. The beautiful scenery of autumn is unobstructed on the mountain, coupled with the blooming flowers, it is extremely romantic.

    Winter: December to February. Incheon’s winter is relatively cold. Because it is located in the coastal area, the surface temperature is slightly higher than that of Seoul. The average temperature in January is around 3.1 degrees Celsius. The Ganghwa Ice Fish Festival is a very popular experience, so I suggest you try it if you have the opportunity.

    Best time to travel

    Spring, summer, and autumn are the best seasons to visit Incheon. When the weather is good, you can go for a walk on the seaside of Wolmido Island and eat delicious seafood in the fall. It is only hot in summer, so you should pay attention to sun protection.
