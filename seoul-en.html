Introduction

    Overview

    Seoul, the capital of the Republic of Korea, has diversified styles. The Blue House and Gyeongbokgung Palace, which are full of Korean traditional architectural styles, can make you feel the ancient charm of the locals; the towering Nanshan Mountain allows you to dominate the scenery of Namsan Park. At Seoul Tower The top overlooks the modern and prosperous city; the shopping sacred places of Myeongdong, Dongdaemun, and Namdaemun are not closed to make you feel refreshed; the traditional food shops, tea houses, and crafts streets of Insadong are a good place for you to experience Korean culture. Place; of course, you can also go outside the city, the Han Riverside blowing wind and Yeouido cherry blossoms are both good choices.

    At the same time, Seoul is a world-famous digital metropolis, so many technical control enthusiasts are vying for this land, and the advanced plastic surgery technology is even more fascinating for women from all over the world. Apart from tourists, tourist attractions such as Apgujeong and Cheongdam-dong are also popular places for many big-name celebrities and wealthy people. And the wealthy Gangnam District is also known to people all over the world because of a Gangnam style.

    Culture

    Seoul is a cultural icon of South Korea, endowed with the charm of multiple levels of culture. Seoul has about 700 museums, such as the National Central Museum and the National Folk Museum, which have a large collection of historical treasures. Moreover, there are nearly 400 various art museums and galleries in various theaters such as Sejong Cultural Center, National Theater, and Art Hall. Visitors can watch various famous works and artworks as they please. In addition, Seoul regularly organizes some large-scale events every year, such as the Seoul Film Festival, the Han River Film Festival, and the gatekeeper handover ceremony of Gyeongbokgung Palace. Tourists can participate according to their interests and itinerary plans.

    Environment

    Seoul is located in the middle of the Korean Peninsula, in a basin, about 30 kilometers from the west coast of the peninsula and about 185 kilometers from the east coast. The city circle of Seoul includes cities such as Suwon and Incheon. The four seasons are distinct and it is very livable. The entire city of Seoul is shown in a clean and tidy image. The Han River passes through the city from here, dividing the city into two parts, the north, and the south, and there are hills about 500 meters above sea level around Seoul, like a natural barrier here.

Tourist time

    Tourist season

    Seoul has a temperate climate with four distinct seasons, with cold and dry winters and hot and humid summers. The average annual temperature is about 11°C, which is lower than other cities at the same latitude, but it will also rise to more than 30°C during summer.

    Spring (March to March): At this time, Seoul is warm and comfortable, and everything is recovering. The most suitable activity is to invite a group of friends out to enjoy the flowers, but the temperature difference between morning and evening is large, so you should add clothes appropriately.

    Summer (June-August): This is a season when locals will choose to go out on vacation because Seoul is extremely hot at this time. However, July is generally the rainy season with heavy rainfall, so visitors should bring umbrellas with them.

    Autumn (September-November): The temperature is mild and the sky is clear. At this time, Seoul is the most suitable time to travel. If you choose to go out at this time, you can see that the mountains around Seoul are full of red leaves, which is very shocking.

    Winter (December-February): The snowy winter in Seoul is very romantic, as shown in many Korean dramas, but the temperature in Seoul is also very low in winter, so tourists should take measures to keep warm.

    Best time to travel

    Seoul travels in all four seasons, and the spring and autumn seasons are particularly comfortable. The cherry blossom season in spring and the golden autumn atmosphere in autumn are the most beautiful times in Seoul. It is also the peak season for tourism in South Korea. The cozy and majestic beauty of the golden autumn in South Korea is worth admiring, and tourists can also enjoy food, shopping, and SPA at the same time. It will be an unforgettable experience if you can spend the local traditional festivals with a Korean friend's home.
